About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Mathematics at Vanderbilt University. My advisor is Dr. Anna Marie Bohmann. I think about equivariant homotopy theory. If you have no idea what any of those words mean, you can look at my short introduction for people who don't do research-level math. You can also check out my collection of math writings.

I previously completed a Master's degree at McGill University under Piotr Przytycki. This resulted in a thesis.

I do a little dancing, a little making music, and a little programming.
